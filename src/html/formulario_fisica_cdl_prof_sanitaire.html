Cinematica 1/2

  • Moto rettilineo uniforme: \[ x(t) = x_0 +v * t, \quad (m, cm) \]
  • Moto uniformemente accelerato: \[ x(t) = x_0 + v_0 * t + \frac{1}{2} a * t^2, \quad v(t) = v_0 + a * t, \quad (\frac{m}{s}, \frac{cm}{s}) \]
  • Moto periodico: \[ f = \frac{1}{T} \quad Hz (s^{-1}) \]

Cienatica 2/2

  • Moto circolare uniforme (\(\phi\) = angolo): \[ \omega = \frac{\phi}{\Delta T} = \frac{2\pi}{T} = 2\pi * f, \quad rad/s \]
    \[ v = \frac{R \phi}{\Delta T} = \omega * R,\quad \text{(m/s, cm/s)} \]
  • Forza: \[ F = m * a, \quad (\frac{Kg * m}{s^2} = Newton; \frac{g * cm}{s^2} = Dyne) \ \]
